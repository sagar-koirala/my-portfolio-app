### Parametric CAD Engineering & Prototyping
A compilation of custom physical enclosures and mechanical assemblies designed for various automation, robotics, and product design projects. Each part was modeled parametrically to allow fast geometric iterations and ensure high-tolerance fits.

### Highlighted Designs
* **Robotics Assemblies:** Modeled complex assemblies including a multi-joint Servo Legged Robot and a rigid mobile PushBot chassis, accounting for center of gravity, actuator torque paths, and component clearances.
* **Functional Hardware:** Designed customized components like the dual Extruder Holder mount, optimizing airflow cooling channels and strain relief for high-speed print head motion.
* **Consumer Concepts:** Engineered detailed product models such as a Thermal Flask cap and assembly, complete with threaded caps and airtight seal gaskets using advanced surface modeling features in SolidWorks.
