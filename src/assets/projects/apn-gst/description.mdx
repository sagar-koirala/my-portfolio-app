### Himalayan Store-and-Forward Telemetry
Designed, manufactured, and deployed an experimental ground sensor terminal to transmit remote environmental data from high-altitude glacial lake basins in Nepal. The primary research focused on validating low-power LoRa links to stream telemetry to the BIRDS-3 CubeSat constellation.

### Link Budget & Antenna Optimization
* **Antenna Design:** Calculated and prototyped high-gain directional antennas to maximize signal gain along the satellite trajectory path.
* **Environmental Enclosure:** Packaged sensitive RF and power boards in IP67-rated weatherproof enclosures with desiccant systems to survive extreme sub-zero mountain environments.
* **Dual-path Failover:** Integrated a backup cellular GSM transceiver alongside the LoRa radio. Because satellite passes were experimental and highly window-constrained, the GSM failover ensured continuous logging and telemetry transmission.
