### Mixed-Signal HDI Design Study
Reverse-engineered the complex High-Density Interconnect (HDI) mainboard of the commercial M5Stack Core2 development module in Altium Designer. The objective was to analyze advanced design strategies for tight component placements and trace layout techniques in commercial-grade consumer IoT devices.

### Technical Analysis & Implementation
* **Microvia Layouts:** Studied the routing and stack-up strategies of multi-layer PCBs utilizing blind and buried microvias to pack the ESP32-D0WDQ6-V3 chip, PMIC, and peripherals into a compact form factor.
* **Mixed-Signal Partitioning:** Inspected the ground isolation barrier and shielding methods separating high-frequency WiFi/Bluetooth RF traces from low-frequency analog sensors and digital serial buses.
* **Power Distribution Network (PDN):** Mapped out the power plane geometry, verifying low-impedance return paths and decouping capacitor networks supplying the core MCU power rails.
